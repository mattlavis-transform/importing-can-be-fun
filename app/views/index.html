Check how to import or export goods into or out of the UK

When to use the service

You can use this service to check what businesses need to have in place at any time during the import or export process, including:

  • before the goods have been bought

  • declaring your goods and moving them across the UK border

  • after the goods have arrived


What the service does

The service will show you the steps you need to take based on where the goods are from and what their commodity code is.

If you don’t know the commodity code for your goods, you’ll be able to search for it in the service.

The steps shown include:

  • registering your business for trading

  • licences and certificates required for your goods

  • calculating taxes for your goods and any reduction in duties

  • declarations and notifications your goods need to clear the UK Border

  • how to reclaim VAT

  • which invoices and records to keep


The steps you need to take will be tailored based on the information you provide, such as where the goods are from and the commodity code of the goods.
